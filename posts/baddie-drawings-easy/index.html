The benefits of coloring: stress relief, mindfulness, creativity Coloring books are not just for kids anymore. In fact, adult coloring books are becoming increasingly popular as a way to wind down after a long day or week. And it’s not just the act of coloring that can be therapeutic, but the designs themselves. “There is something incredibly calming about repetitive patterns and the use of bright colors,” said Dr. Michele Kambolis, a child and family therapist in Vancouver.

Adult coloring books can provide an outlet for stress relief and can be used as a tool for mindfulness and meditation. The act of coloring can help to focus the mind on the present moment and quiet any anxious thoughts that may be running through your head.

And unlike some forms of meditation, coloring is a low-pressure activity that is easy to do even if you’re not feeling particularly Zen.
