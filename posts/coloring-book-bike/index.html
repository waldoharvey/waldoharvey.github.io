What are color pages and why are they important? Color pages are a printing process that is used to produce separate colors of a printed piece of material. They are important because they can be used to create different effects in a design or to help the reader differentiate text and other elements on a page. For example, if you have text that is all in the same color, it can be difficult for readers to read it. By adding different colors to the text, you can make it more visible and easier for people to understand. This is especially helpful when you want to use bold or italic typeface, as these types of changes will be more noticeable with different colors.

Another reason why color pages are important is because they can be used as a way to create an aesthetic for a design.
