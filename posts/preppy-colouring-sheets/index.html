Tips for starting art coloring: Materials, tools, techniques Beginning artists often have a few questions about materials, tools, and techniques for coloring their artwork. Here are some tips to help get you started: -Select the right media: Drawing pencils, crayons, Inks, watercolors can all be used for art coloring. Experiment with different types of media to see what works best for you. -Choose the right colors: When choosing colors for your artwork, consider both the hue and saturation of each color. Hue is how bright or dark a color is and saturation describes how much color is present in a hue.
