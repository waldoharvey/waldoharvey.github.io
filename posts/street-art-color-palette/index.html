The history of adult coloring Adult coloring is a recent trend that has taken off in popularity. Though it may seem like a new fad, the history of adult coloring can be traced back centuries. The first recorded instance of adult coloring dates back to the Middle Ages. During this time, mandalas were created as a form of meditation. Mandalas are circular designs that represent the universe and are often filled with intricate patterns.

Coloring mandalas became popular again in the 1960s during the counterculture movement. Adults would use mandalas as a way to relax and escape from the stresses of everyday life.
